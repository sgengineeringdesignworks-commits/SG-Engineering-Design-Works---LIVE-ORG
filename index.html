About SG Engineering & Design Works

SG Engineering & Design Works is a professional Mechanical & Electrical Design Solutions company providing high-quality engineering design and drafting services for industrial, manufacturing, fabrication, and infrastructure projects.

We specialize in AutoCAD Drafting, 2D Drafting, 3D Modeling, Mechanical Design, Electrical Design, Structural Steel Detailing, MEP Electrical Drafting, Industrial Plant Layouts, PLC & SCADA Drawings, and Engineering Documentation.

Our team delivers accurate and reliable engineering solutions including Fabrication Drawings, Shop Drawings, GA Drawings, Fixture Design, Reverse Engineering, Layout Planning, and Technical Documentation. We help businesses transform concepts and requirements into detailed, production-ready engineering designs.

At SG Engineering & Design Works, we focus on precision, quality, and industry standards to provide practical design solutions that improve project efficiency, reduce development time, and support successful project execution.

With expertise in Mechanical & Electrical Design Solutions, we are committed to delivering innovative, cost-effective, and dependable engineering services that meet our clients' specific project requirements.
